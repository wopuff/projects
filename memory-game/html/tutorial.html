Tutorial

1. Choose the difficulty in the menu. It's recommended to start with the easy mode and work your way up.

2. Remember that you always can go back to the menu by clicking "Memory" at the top.

3. Click a tile to flip it and reveal its image on the other side. The goal is to click two tiles in a row, with the same image. If you don't, the two tiles will turn back and you'll have to remember their images.

4. The goal is to use as few turns as possible. Your stats will be shown at the end of the game. Good luck!
